Privacy Policy for Sensify

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Sensify ("the app", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles information. Short version: Sensify does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Sensify does not require an account and does not ask for any personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or identifiers.

Device sensors

Sensify reads data from your device's hardware sensors purely to make the in-app games work: accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer (compass), ambient light sensor, and device motion. All sensor readings are processed in real time, on your device only. This data is never recorded, stored, sent to us, or shared with any third party.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any.

Analytics and advertising

Sensify contains no third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers. The app works fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

Children's privacy

Sensify does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk of being exposed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
